1. Introduction

Beyond The Limit Studio ("we," "our," or "us") operates the **FastSavers** mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • In-App Purchases: When you make a purchase through Google Play Billing, we process transaction information to verify your premium status. Payment processing is handled securely by Google Play; we do not store your credit card details.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Clipboard Data (Local Only): The app monitors your clipboard locally on your device to facilitate downloading. This logic happens entirely on your device. We do not upload or store your clipboard content on any servers.
  • Usage Analytics: We use Google Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data, including app opens, session duration, screen views, and feature usage. This helps us improve the app experience.
  • Crash Reports: We collect technical logs (via Firebase Crashlytics) if the app crashes to help us fix bugs.
  • Advertising Data: We use Google AdMob to display advertisements to non-premium users. AdMob may collect your device's Advertising ID and general location data.

2.3 Permissions

  • Storage Access: Required to save downloaded videos and photos to your device.
  • Internet Access: Required to download content and display advertisements.
  • Network State: Used to check if you have a stable connection before starting a download.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide the core downloading and saving functionality.
  • Verify and maintain your Premium (Ad-free) status.
  • Monitor and improve app performance and stability.
  • Show relevant advertisements (for free users).
  • Analyze usage trends to plan new features.
