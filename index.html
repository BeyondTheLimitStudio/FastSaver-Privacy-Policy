Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 23, 2025

1. Introduction

Beyond The Limit Studio ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Pinterest Video Saver mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • In-App Purchases: When you make a purchase through Google Play Billing, we process transaction information to verify your premium status. Payment processing is handled securely by Google Play, and we do not store your payment card details.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Analytics: We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data including:
    • App opens and session duration
    • Screen views and navigation patterns
    • Download events and success rates
    • Feature usage statistics
    • Device information (model, OS version, language)
    • Crash reports and performance data
  • Advertising Data: We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:
    • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
    • IP address and approximate location
    • Ad interaction data (views, clicks)
    • Device and network information

2.3 Permissions

The App requires the following permissions:

  • Storage Access: To save downloaded videos to your device
  • Internet Access: To download videos and display advertisements
  • Network State: To check connectivity before downloads

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing and maintaining the App's functionality
  • Processing in-app purchases and verifying premium status
  • Improving app performance and user experience
  • Analyzing usage patterns and app statistics
  • Displaying personalized advertisements (for non-premium users)
  • Detecting and preventing technical issues
  • Complying with legal obligations

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

4.1 Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob uses cookies and similar technologies to provide personalized ads based on your interests.

  • AdMob Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Opt-out of personalized ads: https://adssettings.google.com

4.2 Firebase Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with our App. Firebase collects anonymous usage data and device information.

  • Firebase Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

4.3 Google Play Billing

In-app purchases are processed through Google Play Billing. Google's privacy policy applies to payment processing.

  • Google Play Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Data Retention

  • Analytics Data: Retained by Firebase for up to 14 months
  • Purchase Data: Retained as long as necessary to provide premium features
  • Downloaded Videos: Stored locally on your device until you delete them

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.
